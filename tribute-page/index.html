The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ)

Here is a time line of The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ)

  • 570CE - Born in Makkah
  • 575CE - Looses his parents and becomes an orphan
  • 578CE - Passes into the care of his paternal uncle Abu Talib
  • 595CE - Marries Khadeejah and starts his family life.
  • 610CE - Starts receiving revelation from God Almighty
  • 622CE - Migrates with his followers to Madinah as the oppression became severe
  • 630CE- Returns to Mecca safely
  • 632CE - Dies in Madinah
